Frequently Asked Questions

key

How do I get my API key?

After subscribing, your API key is emailed to you automatically within minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it in your inbox — it comes from noreply@fortress-options.com.

settings

How do I enter my API key in the app?

Open the app, tap the ⚙️ settings icon in the top right, paste your API key in the "API Key" field, and tap Connect. The app will verify your key and connect to the live feed.

swap_vert

Can I change my plan?

Yes. Email us at support@fortress-options.com and we'll upgrade or downgrade your subscription. Changes take effect within one business day.

lock_reset

What if I lost my API key?

Email us at support@fortress-options.com with the email address you used to subscribe and we'll resend your key immediately.

monitoring

What markets does Fortress cover?

Basic covers SPY & QQQ. Pro and Elite add AAPL, AMZN, MSFT, and GOOGL — giving you the full suite of the most liquid options in the market.

cancel

How do I cancel?

Email support@fortress-options.com. We'll cancel your subscription immediately with no questions asked. No hoops, no retention offers — just done.
