How It Works

Three steps. Less than 5 minutes. Then the app finds the plays — you decide whether to take them.

💡

Fortress Options is a research & signal tool — not a broker

This app shows you high-probability options plays — it does not place trades for you. You need your own brokerage account (Robinhood, Tastytrade, TD Ameritrade, IBKR, etc.) to actually enter the trades. Fortress Options tells you what to look at and when to exit — the execution is always yours.

1

Subscribe & Get Your Key

Choose a plan on this page. After payment, your unique API key lands in your inbox within seconds. This key connects the app to your account — keep it safe.

2

Install & Connect

Download from Google Play and install on your Android phone. Open the app — it walks you through entering your API key and setting up a fingerprint or PIN lock. Done in under a minute.

3

Review Plays & Execute in Your Broker

The Plays tab shows ranked credit spreads the scanner found. Review the details — then open your brokerage app (Robinhood, Tastytrade, IBKR, etc.) and enter the trade yourself. Come back to tap Track Trade so Fortress monitors your position.

smartphone

How to Use the App Daily

  • → Pre-market (8–9:30 AM ET) — Open Plays to review overnight scan results. Decide which spreads interest you and prepare your brokerage app.
  • → During market hours — Tap Scan to refresh. See a play you like? Go to your broker (Robinhood, Tastytrade, IBKR, etc.) and place the spread there. Plays update every 30 minutes.
  • → After you place the trade in your broker — Come back to the app and tap Track Trade. Fortress then monitors that position and alerts you when to take profit or cut losses.
  • → At close (3:45–4 PM ET) — Check the Alerts tab. If an exit signal fired, go to your broker and close the position.

When You Get Alerts

Elite subscribers get instant push alerts to Telegram. Here's exactly when notifications fire:

📊

New high-score play found

Fired every 30 min during market hours (9:30 AM – 4 PM ET) when the scanner finds a play scoring 8/10 or higher.

✅

Profit target hit — Take profit

Fires instantly when a tracked position reaches +20% return on max risk. Signal to close for a win.

⚠️

Stop-loss triggered — Cut losses

Fires instantly when a position hits −10% of max risk. Protects you before losses compound.

📅

Expiration warning

Reminder when a tracked position has 2 or fewer days until expiration.

No alerts outside market hours (Mon–Fri, 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET). You won't be woken up at 3 AM.
