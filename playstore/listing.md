## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Fortress Options** brings institutional-grade options intelligence to your Android device. Our AI-powered scanner continuously monitors the market, identifies high-probability setups, and scores every trade from 0 to 10 — so you always know which plays deserve your attention and which ones to skip.

**Stop guessing. Start trading with an edge.**

Most retail traders rely on gut feel, Reddit tips, or stale newsletters. Fortress Options gives you the same systematic, data-driven approach used by professional trading desks — distilled into a clean, mobile-first app built for serious traders.

---

### What Fortress Options Does

- **AI-Scored Plays (0–10):** Every setup is evaluated across multiple dimensions — implied volatility rank, technical structure, risk/reward ratio, and earnings calendar proximity. Only the highest-scoring plays surface to the top.

- **Bull Put Spreads:** The app specializes in defined-risk credit spreads, the preferred strategy of professional income traders. Collect premium with a clear maximum loss — no margin calls, no unlimited downside.

- **Earnings Plays:** Get ahead of the biggest catalyst in options trading. Fortress Options highlights upcoming earnings events with elevated IV, giving you a head start on positioning before the crowd piles in.

- **Real-Time Position Tracker:** Monitor your open spreads from entry to expiration. Track your current P&L, break-even levels, and days to expiration — all in one place.

- **Instant Push Notifications:** Never miss a high-score play. Get notified the moment a new setup clears our scoring threshold, so you can act while the window is open.

- **Biometric Security:** Your trading dashboard is protected by fingerprint authentication. No one gets in but you.

- **Clean Dark UI:** Designed for focused traders who want signal, not noise. The dark interface is easy on the eyes during extended market hours.
