## Required Specifications

### Phone Screenshots
- **Minimum:** 2 screenshots
- **Maximum:** 8 screenshots
- **Required dimensions:** 1080x1920 px (16:9) or 1080x2400 px (20:9)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Orientation:** Portrait

> Tip: Capture at 1080x2400 to cover modern tall-screen phones. The Play Store will scale them for display.

## Feature Graphic

| Asset           | Spec              |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Feature graphic | 1024x500 PNG      |

**What it should look like:**

The feature graphic appears at the top of your Play Store listing on tablets and some phone layouts. Design it as a wide banner with:

- **Background:** Deep dark (#0d0d0d or #111827) matching the app's dark theme
- **Left side:** The Fortress Options shield/logo centered vertically, rendered in gold or amber
- **Right side:** A partial screenshot or mockup of the plays dashboard showing a high-score play card (e.g., score badge "9.2")
- **Center text:** "Fortress Options" in bold white type, with tagline below in smaller gold text: "AI-Scored Options Plays"
- **Accent elements:** Subtle grid lines or candlestick chart graphics in the background at low opacity to suggest a trading/finance context
- **No text near edges:** Keep all text and logos within the center 60% of the image (Play Store crops edges on some devices)

> Create this in Figma, Canva, or Photoshop. Export as PNG at exactly 1024x500 px.
